# **Expression of Interest (EOI)**

## **UNICEF Venture Fund – March 2026 Call**

## **Submitted by: TechFlowSpace.com, Nepal**

## **1\. Startup Overview and Contact Info**

TechFlowSpace.com is a Kathmandu-based frontier technology startup committed to building transparent, inclusive, and privacy-preserving digital infrastructure. We specialize in blockchain and zero-knowledge (ZK) cryptographic technologies to design systems that enhance trust and equity in public services. As a Nepal-registered company, we collaborate closely with NGOs, education stakeholders, and municipal governments to co-create open-source digital solutions aligned with national goals.

## **2\. Problem Statement and Impact on Children**

In Nepal, many children from marginalized and low-income communities struggle to access sustained education due to fragmented service delivery, lack of coordination, and poor visibility into funding and support mechanisms. Despite the availability of public scholarships, midday meals, and learning materials, these services are inconsistently delivered or misallocated, leaving many children unsupported. Teachers and local officials face administrative overload, while donors and NGOs lack visibility into impact. Children’s needs for tuition, uniforms, health care, and psychosocial support often go unmet.

## **3\. Solution Description and Innovation**

TechFlowSpace proposes a holistic education support infrastructure that includes:

\- A privacy-respecting child registry integrated with government and municipal databases using ZKPs

\- A matching engine that pairs children with entitlements, donors, or NGOs

\- Smart contracts for secure, conditional disbursement of funds and services

\- Dashboards for donors, teachers, and government stakeholders

\- Academic monitoring, well-being tracking, and alert triggers for early intervention

\- Mobile tools for teachers to verify services and provide timely support

\- Open APIs to link with Education Management Information Systems (EMIS) and other public records

## **4\. Prototype and Technical Readiness**

We have built:

\- A smart contract-based payment module integrated with verified vendor databases

\- A ZK proof-of-enrollment system for child identity validation

\- Donor and stakeholder dashboards

\- A mockup of a mobile teacher interface

We are engaging with municipal authorities and NGOs in two districts for live pilot deployment in 2026\.
